### KeepScratchPrivacy
A Chrome extension which removes a popup asking users to select their US state in Scratch.

### Percept
A small Cydia tweak for iOS 5-7 that redirects outgoing Siri traffic to ChatGPT. [I originally did this using SiriProxy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wZcufOAslsw).  
<sup>**Unmaintained** &raquo; January 2026 &raquo; [GitHub](https://github.com/calvinkNT/Percept), [Demo/Tutorial Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YGhYX8cglvg)</sup>

### WhatsAppX

<sup>**Unmaintained** &raquo; June-July 2025 &raquo; [GitHub](https://github.com/calvinkNT/WhatsAppX)</sup>
